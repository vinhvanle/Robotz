AZ BUTLER

Detailed Description

Aims

Our ultimate project objective is building a house management system. The system might be considered as a Smart Butler, who has responsibility for not only supervising every circumstance occurring in the household but also maintaining an optimized environment for the employer. Similarly, the system has mainly two factors which are measuring hazard and doing house chores. Specifically, the machine will estimate possible factors that result in a catastrophe such as fire and inform the report to the user to avoid and prevent an accident. Additionally, it will do housework instead of the user. For instance, the product will automatically activate the washing machine by using a typical network between the product and the machine if the machine is full of clothes. The team anticipates that the system will contribute to declining accidents as well as lead to improve the standard of living.

The main objectives of the project are building a machine and application. The first goal was designing the multi-functional machine. The capabilities of the product are detecting fire hazard, leak, dust concentration and invasion of a stranger and doing overall house chores such as washing dishes, cleaning and laundry to maintain organized residence of the customer. To accomplish the objective, the team built a robot having cameras, scanners for recognizing risk factors like smoke and wheels for enhanced mobility. Our team also developed an application which makes a connection between user and the product. By utilizing the application based on the Bluetooth, an individual can receive a report containing assessment from the machine. Moreover, they can control the machine directly to prevent any possible risks considering the report and assign chores in the user-preferred time zone regardless of the time and location.

Building the prototype

Van Vinh used the Arduino Robot to build the prototype. He started buying the robot kit set on linhkientot.vn. He chose the Smart Car KIT Arduino, coming with ultrasonic sensor, remote control module, line tracking module, obstacle avoidance module, motors, wheels, battery, etc. It took the team several hours to build the car and we did not face any challenges. Based on the instructions, we attach pieces and connect parts together in a systematic and seamless way.

1. Link wires with motors and place 4 SG90 motors at 4 corners of a square to assemble the base.

2. Set the base plate onto motors and slide motors mounting through the chassis and link the speed encoders within the motor's inside parts.

3. Embed caster wheel to the chassis

4. Attach wheels to the motors and attach server as well as ultrasonic sensor to the chassis

5. Install the line tracking module

6. Install the Arduino Uno R3 and Shield L293D motor drive chip to the chassis

7. Connect servo pins, motors and ultrasonic sensor to motor drive chip

8. Assemble the battery pack and rocker switch to the chassis and connect them to the motor drive chip

9. Download Arduino IDE, Program Sketch and upload our project to Arduino Uno

There are also 5 analog pins which can be utilized for more sensors, rooms for 2 more motors and a set of servo pins so we can upgrade and enhance the car a bit. The bluetooth module facilitates the connection from the robot to the phone so we can control it. The three LEDs, line tracking modules under the eyes are for following any black tap stuck on the ground.. Certainly, its eyes are the ultrasonic sensor which helps it to know its surroundings and avoid obstacles. The Arduino chip controller is where magic happens. It is the brain of the robot.

The main task, obviously the most challenging and important part is how we make it run, how we make it operate and avoid obstacles. Since we bought the Smart Car Arduino kit, we used the Arduino IDE 1.8.13 provided by Arduino. This open-source tool simplifies the coding process, which is easier for students like us to start and learn coding.

Van Vinh used C programming language to code for the car and its function. He faced a challenge that each wheel will have different controllers and consequently he was having difficulties in finding a way to make it turn left/right at the same time.

Future plan

The robot, as we expect in the long journey, will be built and upgraded to look like a real human. The inner parts are made from steel, aluminum to protect wires, chips and electrical and technical parts. We will include a packing material, which is made of rubber or new material to coat these parts and make them waterproof. The biodegradable materials are used to create a skin like a human, and would be the outer cover to protect the machine from water, fire and other threats. With the shape as a human, the AZ Butler can walk, run, bring things, cook, write, etc. easily and smoothly. And lastly, the next generation will upgrade the robot with Artificial Intelligence to provide the ability to learn and quickly adapt. With Artificial Intelligence, the robot can learn and remember the house map, neighborhood, the family host, friends and relatives, scenarios and many things in such a real life so it can understand and serve our life at the best. First of all, it needs to detect, what is a human, what is a dog, what is a tree, what is an object, the difference between them, etc for the safety of people, pets and the overall environment. The knowledge can be gained and taught by complicated algorithms, codes and information stored in Big Data and Cloud Computing. The robot needs to automatically update and sync with the system to input and output the information. For instance, when it faces a challenge like buying an apple, it needs to retrieve the information of the supermarket, the way to get there, the brand of the apple that the family prefers, the amount, the cost, the way to pick a good apple, etc. If one of the listed information is not in its data, it needs to learn, analyze and try many times by themselves before it can successfully “know” it and put the new lessons into the system. Testing and testing the robot multiple times before it can reach the end-users is very important and essential. Obviously, before launching the product, we have to find investors that have interest in our products and decide to manufacture them in a large number. Then we have to do marketing and campaigns to finally launch the product and sell it to customers.
