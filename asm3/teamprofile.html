Group processes

Ideas and opinions are always fluctuating which is natural for any team projects. The team tried their best in the first week to elevate this at-first-simple robot idea into a more advanced, smart and fully automated butler. However, due to some difference in opinions, the team still struggles to find the right level where they should take this robot to. Around the second week of the project, a new member was introduced into the team which is an enormous help and support for the team. Although the new member is Korean, there were no language barriers between the teammates and we managed to work together flawlessly. Towards the end of the semester, the team became surprisingly more productive through good communication and motivation. Everyone worked hard to finish a detailed report, intriguing video presentation and a working prototype.

If the ASM3 could be extended, we believe the advancement of the project as well as the level of practicality of the report will be enhanced as we have more time to discuss, debate and understand each other’s working habits.
